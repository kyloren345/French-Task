Backstory

The French language was established in 842 A.D. (After Death) in it's old form know as the Strasbourg Oaths which was established with sworn oaths in French and German

French was also a growing language at the time and was mainly coming from Vulgar Latin which is the Romance version of Latin considering that French is a Romance language itself.

FUN FACT The Palace of Versailles was created for the first time originally in 1624 by Jacques Lemercier who was entrusted by the King at the time Louis XIII (Louis the Thirteenth). However the palace was rebuilt in 1643-1647 to suit what the King Louis XIV (Louis the Fourteenth) had standardised for what he wanted in the palace as it became his residence after former King Louis XIII passed away in 1631.
