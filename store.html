Backstory

Learning French allows you to be able to have a deeper understanding of what French as a language has undergone to be the French variations that we know of today

French is also a language that takes a lot more into account for than just being a descendant of Vulgar Latin and the Gallo-Romance category specifically. French also has a lot of set boundaries to speaking properly just like in English how we can't say Me was… we say I was… the same thing applies to French but in a different way, for example Je manger un pomme instead of Je manger une pomme, the difference is that one doesn't have the infamous French way of looking at objects in feminine and masculine. Un is the masculine version of a, an, one and more. Same thing with une it can mean a, an, but not one.
